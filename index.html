ABOUT TIDE PROJECTS

Who We Are

Tide Projects is a trusted provider of qualified maintenance services across commercial, industrial and public sectors, contributing meaningfully to New Zealand's civil structures and reinforcing the fabric of its communities.

With a deep respect for the nation's heritage and culture, we are committed to preserving what makes New Zealand unique. For over a decade we've proudly supported and served local communities, delivering Interior & Exterior Decorating, Special Coating Systems, Metal Fabrication & Installation, and Urban Maintenance — every project carried out with care, compliance and craftsmanship.

We specialise exclusively in the commercial sector — proudly maintaining Government and Council facilities, Schools and Universities, large-scale Commercial Buildings, and Hospitals, making maintenance hassle-free and delivering results built to last.

HEALTH & SAFETY

Safety is foundational to how we operate.

We are deeply committed to protecting our team, our contractors, and the wider community through a proactive, systems-based approach. As part of our commitment to a safe and responsible workplace, random drug testing is conducted among employees.

Comprehensive Risk Management

A rigorous process to identify, assess, and control health, environmental, and safety risks across all project phases.

Active Site Oversight

Regular site inspections, both scheduled and unannounced, to monitor compliance and reinforce safe practices.

Safe Work Method Statements

Detailed SWMS developed for each task, outlining procedures, hazards, and control measures.

Mandatory PPE & Training

Full PPE is mandatory on all sites, with comprehensive induction and ongoing training for every team member.

Critical Risk Culture

Fostering a mindset to proactively identify, manage, and verify potential hazards before any work commences — across every daily operation.
